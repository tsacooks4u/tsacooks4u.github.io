About

Namaste! I am Taruni Syamala and welcome to my food blog. I will be posting my recipes here to share it with the world. If you enjoy any of the recipes, feel free to leave a comment.

I have been passionate about food since I was very young. I have several food memories from my travels, outstanding dishes that left a mark on my brain. I try to recreate these dishes and also invent new recipes.
